About Me

In last four and a half years, I've been focused on GPGPU chip design with a group of tremendous guys. I took part in the architecture design, digital logic implementation of several key blocks/subsystems, entire chip integration, and also took the role of projects management and execution.

Luckily, we have made the great achievements that two chips Tape-out successfully, of which the first one has already stepped into the mass production stage and shipped to our customer such as telecom operator and data center for cloud computation.

Besides the GPGPU period, I've been in IC design industry for over 16 years, mainly focused on chip and system design of communication and SoC topics with multiple roles, and shipped various chips and products for communication, embedded system, mobile, data center.
